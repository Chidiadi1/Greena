Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our services. For further assistance, reach out to our customer support team.

How Can I Change My Password Easily

Go to your account settings, click on "Security", and select "Change Password". Follow the prompts, and you'll receive an email confirmation once your password has been successfully updated.

How Do I Change My Subscription Plan?

Navigate to the "Subscription" section in your account dashboard. From there, you can select a new plan and you'll receive a confirmation email with the updated details.

How Do I Change My Dashboard Photo?

Go to your profile, click on the current photo, and select "Upload New Photo". Changes will be reflected on your dashboard instantly.
